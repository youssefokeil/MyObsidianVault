## 2) Factorizing Trinomials
### 2.1 Factorizing $x^2+bx+c$  ($x^2$ مفيش رقم جمب ال)
**Steps:** 
Example: $x^{2}+6x+8$
1. Put two brackets of the form 
---> 	$(x\hspace{1em})(x\hspace{1em})$
2. Factorize the trinomial to get product  $c$ (x الرقم اللي مجمبوش), and sum $b$ (الرقم اللي جمبه x)

| product = 8 | sum = 6 |
| ----------- | ------- |
| 1*8         | 9       |
| ==2*4==     | ==6==   |
---> the two numbers are 2,4

---> $(x+2)(x+4)$
