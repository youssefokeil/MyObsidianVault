# Capacitance Combinations
- Series 
![[series-cap.png|250]]
$$X_{C}=X_{1}+X_{2}+X_{3}$$
$$\frac{1}{C_{eq}}=\frac{1}{C_{1}}+\frac{1}{C_{2}}+\frac{1}{C_{3}}$$
- Parallel
![[parall-cap.png|250]]
$$X_{C}=\frac{1}{X_{1}}+\frac{1}{X_{2}}+\frac{1}{X_{3}}+...$$
$$C_{eq}=C_{1}+C_{2}+C_{3}+...$$
